Planning Your Visit

üöó Getting Here

From Kigali, take the main road north toward Musanze. Just before reaching the city, turn off toward Nyange village. The journey takes approximately 2 hours by car. Local motorcycle taxis (motos) are available from Musanze.

ü¶ç Combine With Gorilla Trekking

Many visitors combine a trip to Volcano Arts with gorilla trekking at nearby Volcanoes National Park. We're happy to help coordinate your visit around your trekking schedule.

üë• Group Tours

We welcome tour groups and can arrange special experiences including artist meet-and-greets, traditional craft demonstrations, and cultural presentations. Contact us in advance to arrange.
