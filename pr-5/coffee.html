Our Coffee Story

Rwanda is home to some of the world's finest coffee, grown in the rich volcanic soil of our highlands. Our coffee shop exclusively serves beans from cooperative farms in the Northern Province, where smallholder farmers practice sustainable growing methods passed down through generations.

By choosing our coffee, you support a chain of positive impact‚Äîfrom the farmers who grow the beans, to the roasters who perfect them, to our artists who create in this welcoming space. It's community in a cup.
