Our Mission

Building a sustainable future for Kinigi through art, community, and opportunity.

Art That Changes Lives

Volcano Arts & Hospes was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to use the creative spirit of our community to build a better future for everyone in Nyange village. Nestled in the shadow of Rwanda's majestic volcanic mountains, our village has long been home to talented artists and craftspeople whose work deserves to be shared with the world.

Every purchase from our gallery directly supports local families, provides training and employment, and helps preserve the artistic traditions that have been passed down through generations. The Story of Nyange

Kinigi is a small village in Rwanda's Northern Province, surrounded by the breathtaking Virunga volcanic mountains. For generations, our community has been known for its skilled artisans‚Äîpainters who capture the beauty of our landscape, weavers who create intricate baskets, and craftspeople who keep the tradition of Imigongo art alive.

But like many rural communities, Kinigi has faced challenges. Young people often left for Kigali in search of employment. Those with disabilities struggled to find meaningful work. Traditional art forms risked being lost as fewer people carried on the craft.

Volcano Arts & Hospes was created to address these challenges. By connecting our artists with customers around the world, we've built a sustainable enterprise that keeps our community strong and our traditions alive.

The Cooperative Model

Volcano Arts & Hospes is owned and operated by its member artists. Every artist has a voice in how we run our organization, and profits are shared fairly among all members. This cooperative structure ensures that the benefits of our success flow directly to the people who create our beautiful artworks.
